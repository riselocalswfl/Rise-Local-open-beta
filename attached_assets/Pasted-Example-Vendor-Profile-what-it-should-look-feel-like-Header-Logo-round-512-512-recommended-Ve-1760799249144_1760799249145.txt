About / Bio

We’re the Knight family behind Sunshine Grove Farm—three acres of seasonal veggies, pasture-raised eggs, and small-batch honeys. We farm regeneratively: no synthetic pesticides, heavy composting, cover crops, and pollinator habitat. Our goal is to keep food dollars in SWFL and make it easy for busy families to eat fresh, local produce.

Content tips vendors will see when filling this out

Bio: 80–160 words, mission → practices → what to buy → how to get it.

Logo: square PNG/SVG, 512×512 min; avoid text-heavy marks.

Photos: 6–10 high-res (minimum 1200px wide), mix of people + products; 1 short vertical video (10–20s).

Values: Only pick what you can prove (upload docs or add proof notes).

Sourcing %: Show your exact % for each product when possible; weekly average on profile.
